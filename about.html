About

Hello! I'm a founder based in San Francisco building Graph with Brad. I've built products for Twitch, SoundCloud, and Amazon with stints in venture capital (8VC) and the federal government.

I was born in Huntsville, Alabama and raised in the suburbs of Atlanta, Georgia. I'm an alumni of the University of Georgia. I'm a programmer, designer, and product-builder.

I create for humans. I am drawn to multi-disciplinary perspectives, to liberal arts, to celebrating creativity and art and beauty, to making humans feel like they are living to their full potential.

Therefore, I build products for people to connect on a visceral level. I've done this in many ways throughout my career — helping creators share their art with fans, helping people create communities on the internet, and even investing in entrepreneurs that dare to challenge the status quo.

Most of my time is now spent thinking and building Graph, but I love to code and build side projects. I'm always looking for talented people to join me on this journey.

If you'd like to get in touch, please email me or message me on X.
